# Research Plan for Implementing AMD (ROCm) Compatibility in AirLLM

## Introduction
This document outlines a research plan for implementing AMD (ROCm) compatibility in AirLLM. The goal is to ensure that AirLLM can efficiently leverage AMD GPUs for optimized performance.

## Research Objectives
1. **Understanding ROCm Architecture**: Familiarize with the ROCm platform and its architecture.
2. **Integration Strategies**: Explore various strategies for integrating ROCm with AirLLM.
3. **Performance Benchmarking**: Establish benchmarks to evaluate performance improvements and compatibility.
4. **Community and Contribution**: Engage with the community for feedback and contributions.

## Methodology
- Conduct a literature review on ROCm compatibility in machine learning frameworks.
- Analyze existing integration practices.
- Develop prototypes to test ROCm integration in a controlled environment.
- Document findings and iterate based on feedback.

## Timeline**
- **Week 1-2**: Literature review and initial analysis.
- **Week 3-4**: Prototype development.
- **Week 5**: Benchmarking and testing.
- **Week 6**: Documentation and review.

## Conclusion
This research plan serves as a roadmap to achieve full ROCm compatibility in AirLLM, enhancing the accessibility and performance for users leveraging AMD hardware.
